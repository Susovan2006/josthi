Basics FAQ

What is Josthi.com? + −

Josthi.com is a web based application, ment for those who stay away from their parents and near and dear ones, but still want to take care of them remotely. You can gift your parents a josthi service and they will enjoy it.

What are the areas Josthi is currently operational? + −

Josthi.com is a brand new startup launched on 1st Jan 2021. We are targeting mainly in the Kolkata area. Gradually we will expand. But you are always free to contact us and know the coverage area.

How reliable is this App and the Service? + −

Josthi is an app where you don’t need to store any credit card info. We will need your address and contact details to provide the services, but they will never be shared by any one.

Josthi team will never enter your house, all will be done in the door step. The service providers are verified ones, but still if you have your preferences we can arrange accordingly.

My Parents are old and might not have access to the app, is the on call facility available? + −

Yes, 24X7 oncall support will be available, for any queries or concern, they can directly call the agents.

How to avail the free services? + −

Please call us, we will guide you with the 15 days trial service. Provided we have service provided in that area.

Can I get on demand services? + −

Yes specific on demand services are available, you will have to purchase the service and then the agent will assist you.

Can I get a refund, if i am not satisfied with the service? + −

Sure, customers are our first priority, If you would like to terminate the plan we will get the refund for the remaining days. But I am sure , you don’t need to.

After Registration what are the things I need to do? + −

After the registration process, please make sure to fill out your personal details, put as much info as you can. In case of emergency the contact details are required.
Add the beneficiary you would like to add to the service.
Allow 3-4 Business days, someone will get back to you and assign an agent.(If Agent is available in that area)
Now go ahead and purchase your plan.
Keep the agent details handy, for any problem, call them directly. Or you can open a ticket.

Who are the Agents? + −

There are many people in Kolkata and WestBengal and they would like to serve the community and earn some money. Josthi is a platform to facilitate that opportunity. Anyone willing to provide the service to the end users can be an Agent, but the Agent has to undergo an interview and background verification process. Need to provide all the necessary documentation. Only after proper validation, an Agent can be operational.
