Let's Start with a small Story and you will know the History.

I am an NRI residing in the USA, far away from my parents and relatives. My husband is an IT professional in an US based Company, so it’s not possible for us to visit them frequently. Sometimes I feel concerned about them but there is now way I would help them, nowadays you have so many online shopping sites in India, that ease my life a bit. I send them grocery and daily needs, but i was still looking for some service provider who would help my near and dear ones in day to day needs. I searched a lot and there was none. There are so many start-ups and large companies , but no one ever thought of the old ones. I had a thought of setting up a start-up but being a homemaker, I didn't have that confidence. So i was used to the situation..

Then came 2020, a year the entire world would remember for decades. It was COVID-19, spreading like wildfire and every one was in panic, not sure what to do. Few words became very common among all, like lockdown, work from home, mask , sanitizer etc. The entire world was a standstill with lockdown. Being in the US, we had the privilege to get the basic essential commodities, but it was a nightmare for me when I would think about the situation in India , West Bengal. No one was prepared for a lockdown. What would happen to my parents, in-laws and relatives who are elderly. How would they get the basic service? So this time the plan for the startup sprung up once again. I saw my husband was doing work from home, so I was sure he would give me some time for my start-up. After all he is an IT professional, I know I would get technical help from him.

The journey began, I started learning HTML 5, JavaScript and some basic technologies. Created a plan, My husband helped me with the Server setup and all. With the help of a good friend landed with this josthi.com.

I know there will be many people like me, away from their parents and relatives and facing the same problem. This just a start to see how we can serve our loved ones remotely. Still a long way to go, but with your support, I am sure, one day I will become successful and bring back the smile to thousands and thousands of parents in our native land.
